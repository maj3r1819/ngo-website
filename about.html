Meet the board of Trustees.

We engage with the Veer Naris not as ‘donors’ but as a concerned ‘family’ and as a small effort to join them in their difficult lonely journey.

Air Marshal Bhushan Gokhale, PVSM,AVSM,VM

An illustrious combat pilot of the India Airforce. He has always been known to have the welfare of the soldier as his priority. After his retirement he remains an advisor to the Government on critical security issues. He has also devoted himself to various educational and charity organisations and he provides direction and impetus to the Trust.

Abhay Desai

A reputed businessman of Pune with a deep empathy to anyone in need. He has devoted considerable time and resources towards various charitable causes. Moved by the sacrifices of our soldiers in protecting us, he has taken on the mission of spreading the word as a debt to the martyrs.

Anand Madiwale

Graduated from Bombay University with post-graduation in Administrative Management from Jamnalal Bajaj Institute of Management, affiliated to Bombay University. Additionally he qualified in the Quality Control of Drugs from Hafkin Institute, Bombay. Throughout his career in the Central Government he had a close view of National security and of the Forces ensuring it. He brings in a close understanding of the difficulties faced by Veer Naris and is dedicated to helping them

Sushama Bhide

Wife of an army officer and has spent over 40 years in the Army environment working with wives of Jawans in her various capacities. She has an intimate understanding of the challenges faced by the Veer Naris and has the capabilities to earn their trust and reach out to them.

Col Dilip Abhaynkar

. An experienced army officer and an able administrator. After retiring from the Army he has continued to work for the welfare of retired soldiers and their families. He has valuable insights into the government provisions and actions required to ensure that the benefits reach the Veer Naris.

Rohit Joshi

An innovator who is partnering a successful artificial intelligence oriented start-up, he has committed respect to those in uniform. A nationalist to the core, he has taken the responsibility to helm the administrative end of the trust as the Managing Trustee - a valuable member of the team.

Siddharth Date

- A young dynamic entrepreneur, running a highly successful business. A young leader, has been active for social causes in organisations like Rotaract , Pugmarks and a few NGO’s as well ! Vastly networked, he takes special interest in reaching out to people for the cause of Veer Naris towards whom his concern is in his DNA.

Meet our Advisors.

We select children after verification from Army sources as well as from the school the child studies in and support them through their entire education. We intend to also guide and mentor them through their academic and vocational journey.

Maj Gen Sanjay Bhide YSM, VSM

A special forces officer who has served in Kashmir for over a decade. He is closely associated with welfare of veterans and has dedicated himself to addressing difficulties faced by Veer Naris. He advises the trust in verification of details sent to the trust..

Dr Chandrashekhar Karve

CEO of a reputed hospital chain, he is a staunch Nationalist and brings with him the passion and drive to help Veer Naris. Experienced in managing large Trusts, his advise on administrative matters too is valuable.
