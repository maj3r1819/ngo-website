Objective Of the Trust.

To support children’s education of martyrs of Indian Armed Forces, Central Police and Para Military Forces, State Police and any civilians who lay down their lives to protect the Nation from external and internal enemies.

In past decade over 4000 personnel of the security forces have made the supreme sacrifice while fighting enemies of India. The trust seeks to repay the society’s debt towards these martyrs - the real Heroes of the Nation. We select children after verification from Army sources as well as from the school the child studies in and support them through their entire education. We intend to also guide and mentor them through their academic and vocational journey.

Vision of the Trust.

The Nation faces myriad threats both external and internal. These threats are a reality of the modern world. To ensure security of the Nation, enabling each one of us to prosper and engage in any activity of our choice, Armed forces of our country are called upon to defend the country’s interests. In accomplishing this mission given to them by us, many of them make the supreme sacrifice beyond the call of duty. It is to these brave men that we owe our freedom. We, as a society, must express our gratitude to these soldiers of our Nation, and humbly bow our head in prayer. The widows of these brave men – the Veer Naris – continue to face the world with stoic courage.

Process

Since 2017, we have joined hands with Veer Naris to help & encourage their children through their academic journey from Pre Primary to Post Graduation. We obtain details of martyrs from various offices of the Army or the relevant Force. This is the first step on accuracy of the data. We then contact the Veer Naris and guide them in the process of application. The process is kept simple and it can be easily completed even if she is in a remote rural area. Scholarship amount is decided based on a yardstick approved by all Trustees and funds are released to the Veer Nari’s bank account. All donors are kept apprised of the disbursal of scholarships on regular basis.
